Getting Started

  1. Click Add to VCC below to add this package listing
  2. In VCC, add Outfit Save System to your avatar project
  3. In Unity, go to Terrorized/Tools/Outfit Save System at the top of your screen
  4. Enter your license key and click Verify
  5. Select your avatar, configure your save slots, and click Generate
